Opeyemi Taiwo Quadri

Opeyemi Taiwo, the C.E.O of Sure Percussions Entertainment is a versatile talking drummer specializing in playing and teaching various Yoruba drums such as Gángan (Talking Drum), Òmèlè, Gúdúgúdú, Ìyá Ìlù, Bàtá and many more. He excels in Fuji, Juju, Gospel, studio works, and more. Opeyemi also builds, sells and repairs local drums and trains individuals in the art of drumming and drum-making.
